## LLDB

![[Pasted image 20260319112828.png]]

>[!note]- Explanation  
> - Switched to **Intel syntax** for easier reading  
> - Set breakpoint at `main` and inspected disassembly  
> - Found interesting call:  
> ```  
> lea rax, [rbp-0x110]  
> mov rdi, rax  
> call decode_password  
> ```  
> - This shows:  
> - buffer at `[rbp-0x110]`  
> - passed into `decode_password()`  
> - so decoded password will be stored there
